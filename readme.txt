Send mail to your customer and upsell your product!

== Description ==

This plugin allow to easly contact customer that have **purchase** a specific product.
When edit a product, just swich on "Activate notification email" and click on update.
All your customer that have purchased this product will be notified by an mail with update. See video and image below.

https://youtu.be/aauVapCC4bw


*We test our plugins with many other. If you find incompatibility with one specific plugin, please inform us at <a href="mailto:report@nsthemes.com">report@nsthemes.com</a>.*


== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire 'ns-send-mail-to-update-version-plugin' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. View admin menu, then click on 'Add Prod' and start to customize it.
